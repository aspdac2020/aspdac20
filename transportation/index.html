Transportation (Direction to China National Convention Center)

Map

Click to see the full-size image.

From Beijing Capital International Airport (PEK)

Distance: 26KM

  • Take subway Airport Express Line, and transfer to Line 10 at Sanyuanqiao station, and then transfer to Line 8 at Beitucheng station. Get out at Exit H of Olympic Green station (approx. 70 minutes).
  • By taxi, it will take 40 minutes, fare from CNY 80.

From Beijing Daxing International Airport (PKX)

Distance: 86KM

  • Take subway Daxing International Airport Express Line, and transfer to Line 10 at Caoqiao station, and then transfer to Line 8 at Beitucheng station. Get out at Exit H of Olympic Green station (approx. 90 minutes).
  • By taxi, it will take 80 minutes, fare from CNY 150.

From Beijing Railway Station

Distance: 15KM

  • Take subway Line 2, and transfer to Line 8 at Guloudajie station. Get out Exit H of Olympic Green station (approx. 50 minutes).
  • By taxi, it will take 35 minutes, fare from CNY 45.

From Beijing West Railway Station

Distance: 18KM

  • Take subway Line 9, and transfer to Line 6 at Baishiqiaonan station, and then transfer to Line 8 at Nanlouguxiang station. Get out at Exit H of Olympic Green station (approx. 55 minutes).
  • By taxi, it will take 40 minutes, fare from CNY 55.
